Interesting information about penguins

  • Penguins can drink seawater due to a special gland that filters out salt.
  • Emperor penguins endure extreme cold, huddling together in large colonies for warmth.
  • Some penguin species can leap several feet out of the water, showcasing their impressive agility.
  • Gentoo penguins often propose to their mates with pebbles, a charming courtship ritual.
